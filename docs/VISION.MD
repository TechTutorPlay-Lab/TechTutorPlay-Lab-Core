# Vision & Mission 🔭

## Vision
To democratize access to high-level IT skills by integrating Artificial Intelligence into the learning process, making complex technology accessible to everyone.

## Mission
To build a bridge between traditional education and the professional software engineering world through AI-assisted tools, hands-on experimentation, and high-performance computing.

## Core Values
* **Innovation:** Constantly exploring the boundaries of AI (Vertex AI, Copilot).
* **Quality:** Professional-grade code developed on high-end hardware (RTX 5070/7900X3D).
* **Open Learning:** Making the development process transparent and educational.
